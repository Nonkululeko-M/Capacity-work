ABOUT US

Snesh's Bakery is one of Surrey's few long-standing Craft Bakeries.

We have built our reputation on combining excellent traditional heating
with a fantastic value.

We provide our customers with a complete selection of bread, forte bread,
morning foods, cakes, and baked goods.

At our East Horsley pastry shop, our bread is cooked every day before being
sent to our twelve stores. We use extraordinarily talented specialist dough
punchers to ensure that every loaf of bread is consistently perfect.

In order to ensure that the loaf of bread you buy at the beginning of the day
is crisp out of the stove, our pastry chefs labor at that time.
Our commitment to quality and taste has made us a favorite choice for families
around the world.
